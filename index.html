Gitpazo — IT professional, parent, and occasional overthinker about technology.

GitHub

This site exists because I kept giving people advice I wasn't sure existed anywhere in a simple form.

On the family side — I'd watch parents hand phones to kids as pacifiers, then wonder why they couldn't get them back. I've done it myself. The research on this is clear but the honest conversation between parents is rare. So I built a tool to start it.

On the work side — I spent years working with Microsoft Entra ID, making mistakes, finding fixes, and realising the documentation rarely tells you what to do when something goes wrong in the real world. I'm not the most senior person in the room. But sometimes that's exactly who you need to hear from.

Nothing here is sponsored. Nothing is behind a login. If something helps you, share it with someone it might also help.
